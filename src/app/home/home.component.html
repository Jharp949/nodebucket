House Nodebucket

Nestled within the towering spires of Nodebucket School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, the Nodebucket household exudes an air of intellectual curiosity and creative brilliance. Home to the house known for its wit and wisdom, Ravenclaw fosters a community of scholars, thinkers, and innovators.
